## What Makes a Great Personal Statement?

One of the most common questions students ask is: "What makes a personal statement stand out?" While there’s no one-size-fits-all answer, the key is to be authentic, clear, and focused. Your personal statement should:

- Demonstrate your passion for the field and the program you're applying to.
- Explain how your background has prepared you for success in graduate school.
- Clearly articulate your goals and how the program aligns with those goals.
